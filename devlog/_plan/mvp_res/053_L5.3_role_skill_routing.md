## Scope (decision-complete)

  - `plugins/codexclaw/agents/reviewer.toml`
  - `plugins/codexclaw/agents/executor.toml`
- Explorer behavior:
  - read-only codebase investigation.
  - consults `dev`, `dev-architecture`, `dev-debugging`, `dev-backend`, and `dev-frontend`.
  - outputs direct answers with file:line evidence.
- Reviewer behavior:
  - adversarial read-only review.
  - consults `dev-code-reviewer`, `dev-security`, `dev-architecture`, and `dev-testing`.
  - outputs `VERDICT: PASS | FAIL` with blockers and notes.
- Executor behavior:
  - bounded write work inside an assigned scope.
  - consults `dev` plus surface routers such as frontend, backend, testing, scaffolding, debugging.
  - reports files touched and verification run.
- Must-NOT-Have:
  - No writes by explorer or reviewer.
  - No destructive git commands.
  - No scope expansion by executor without surfacing it.
