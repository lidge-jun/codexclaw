## Reinforcement shape

1. Put a small classifier at the top of `cxc-search`: latest/current fact, official-doc fact, implementation/source fact, comprehensive research.
2. Tighten Tier 2 proof language around date capture, primary-source bias, corroboration, and conflict handling.
3. Turn `skill-hub` from a passive catalog intro into a decision rubric with 6-8 common routing forks.
4. Promote `ast-grep` to a first-class trigger in upstream routing text, not only inside its own usage page.
5. State one explicit ownership law: `search` owns current/external/public evidence policy; other skills point to it and do not duplicate the ladder.
6. Add one short cross-skill mandate: when material facts may have changed, load `search` before answering from memory.
