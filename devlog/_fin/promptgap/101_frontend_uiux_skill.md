Gap class: PROMPT/SKILL · evidence: explorer #101

> codexclaw's frontend stack is much stronger than omo's old single-skill aesthetic prompt, but
> the **skill bodies still under-carry a few high-frequency jaw/cli-jaw disciplines**. The

## Reinforcement shape

1. Resolve the body-level contradiction first: one ambiguity policy, not a 6-question ladder in one section and a 1-question rule in another.
2. Promote the most reusable UX-state contract into `dev-uiux-design` itself so the trigger words "onboarding / empty / error / loading" carry actual behavioral force before ref-reading.
3. Move three mechanical frontend review stubs into `dev-frontend` body: hero discipline, common AI-tell blacklist, CTA/form micro-checks.
4. Add a typography stance and IA chooser to `dev-uiux-design` so font and nav decisions stop being pure taste vibes plus ref pointers.
5. Keep deep catalogs in references, but make the router bodies carry the **minimum non-optional scanner** a reviewer or executor should remember after one read.
